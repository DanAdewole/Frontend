About Us

Why We Exist?

PowerUp was created to address the pressing need for affordable and sustainable access to electricity and light in communities in need. We believe that access to electricity and light is a basic human right and that the lack of access to these resources is a major barrier to economic and social development. Our goal is to help communities in need overcome this barrier by connecting them with financing and technical support to install renewable energy solutions.

Our Objectives

  1. To provide a source of revenue for communities, NGOs, and individuals.
  2. To enhance the provision of electrical gadgets to homes and communities.
  3. To simplify the financing process for renewable energy projects in communities in need.
  4. To connect communities with potential partners who can provide the necessary financial and technical support to install renewable energy solutions.
