Why We Exist

Energizer was created to address the pressing need for affordable and sustainable access to electricity and light in communities in need. We believe that access to electricity and light is a basic human right and that the lack of access to these resources is a major barrier to economic and social development. Our goal is to help communities in need overcome this barrier by connecting them with financing and technical support to install renewable energy solutions.
